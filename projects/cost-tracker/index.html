Cost Tracker App

Minimal cost tracker app

A beautifully simple app to track your regular expenses, designed for iOS with privacy and ease-of-use in mind.

  • ✅ Minimal UI: Focus on what matters—your expenses.
  • 🔒 Private by design: All data stays on your device or iCloud.
  • 📊 Quick insights: See your monthly and yearly spending at a glance.
  • 🌙 Dark mode: Looks great day and night.
  • 📱 iOS-native: Fast, smooth, and feels right at home on your iPhone.
Privacy Policy

Design Philosophy

Cost Tracker is for people who value simplicity and focus. Inspired by iOS design, it’s a truly native-feeling app that does one thing—track your regular expenses—beautifully and efficiently. There are no ads, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees: just a single, one-time purchase. I believe finance apps should be clutter-free, so I won’t add features that distract from the core purpose. No bloat, no distractions—just the essentials, done right.

Roadmap

  • 🔄 iCloud sync: Sync your expenses across all devices
  • 🔔 Customizable notifications: Get gentle reminders for upcoming expenses.
  • 🛠️ Multiple accounts: Track expenses for multiple accounts.

I only add features that truly improve the core experience—no clutter, no bloat.
